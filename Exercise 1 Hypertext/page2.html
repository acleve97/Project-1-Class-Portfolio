Hydrangeas

Cultivation


The hydrangea genus is highly adaptable to a range of scenarios. Many of the species are garden plants that thrive in moist and rich soil
It is recommended to plant hydrangeas in a place that has less sun, so that the harshest sun during the day isn't hurting the plant.
