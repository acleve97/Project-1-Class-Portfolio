Hydrangeas

In Media



The last page told us about the significance of the Hydrangea, on this page, we're going to talk about Hydrangeas in media.
Because hydrangeas are very popular in Japan, and so is rainy season, you can expect many games with Japanese roots to feature hydrangea themed content.
One of the games I play, Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp, almost always has a rainy season gacha reward in late May or June. Because of them being my favorite flower
I'm always so tempted to make in game purchases to get the items. Going down the list of events in games I was able to find we have: The BanG Dream! Girls Band Party,
Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp, Pikmin Bloom, and a screenshot of what looks to be a hydrangea themed character from Cookie Run.
